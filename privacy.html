Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Who we are

Halls & Wallets is an independent, UK-wide student deals website. This page explains what data we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to understand overall site traffic (for example, how many people visit and which pages are popular). We don't use this information to identify you personally, and we don't sell data to third parties.

When you first visit, you'll be asked to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Declining won't affect your ability to use the site — it only limits our ability to see anonymised traffic patterns.

You can clear your cookie choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain, which will show the cookie banner again.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click through and take out a product (for example, switch bank account or sign up to a broadband provider), we may earn a commission from the provider. This never affects:

  • The price or terms you get from the provider
  • Which deals we choose to list
  • How we rank or describe any offer

Links that may earn a commission are marked with an "Ad" label next to the button.

What we don't do

  • We don't ask for or store your bank details, National Insurance number, or any financial account information
  • We don't sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • We don't track you across other websites

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email hello@hallsandwallets.co.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page as the site grows (for example, if we add new tools or local city data). We'll update the "last updated" date above whenever we do.
